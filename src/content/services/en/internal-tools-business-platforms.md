## When Off-the-Shelf Doesn't Fit

Generic SaaS tools work until they don't. At some point, your business processes become too specific, your data too interconnected, or your team too large for spreadsheets and workarounds.

That's when a custom internal platform becomes the highest-leverage investment you can make: **a system built around how your business actually works**, not how a SaaS vendor thinks it should.

## What We Typically Build

- **Operations dashboards** that pull data from 5+ sources into one view
- **Booking/scheduling platforms** for businesses with complex resource allocation
- **Client portals** that give customers self-service access to their data
- **Workflow systems** that digitize paper-based or email-based approval processes
- **Data management tools** that replace the spreadsheet that "only Sarah knows how to use"

## Built to Last

We build with modern, maintainable technology stacks (Symfony, Vue.js, PostgreSQL, AWS) and ensure your platform can be maintained by any competent development team - not just us. No vendor lock-in.
