## AI That Actually Works

The AI landscape is noisy. Every vendor promises transformation. Most deliver a chatbot.

We take a different approach: **start with the business problem, not the technology**. We map your workflows, identify the bottlenecks, and apply automation - including AI - only where it delivers measurable value.

## Typical Automation Wins

The biggest ROI usually comes from automating tasks that are:
- **High-volume and repetitive** - data entry, report generation, invoice processing
- **Rule-based with exceptions** - where AI handles the 80% and humans handle the edge cases
- **Cross-system** - copying data between tools, syncing records, triggering actions

## Our Approach

1. **Map** your current processes and pain points
2. **Score** automation opportunities by effort vs. impact
3. **Build** the highest-value automations first
4. **Iterate** based on real-world results

We don't sell AI as a silver bullet. Some processes are better solved with a simple script or a better SaaS tool. We'll tell you when that's the case.
