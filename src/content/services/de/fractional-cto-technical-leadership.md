## Why Fractional CTO?

Most growing businesses reach a point where technology decisions become too important to leave to chance - but too early to justify a full-time CTO at €200k+/year.

A fractional CTO gives you **senior technical leadership on your terms**: a few days a month, a focused engagement, or an ongoing advisory relationship. You get 20 years of architecture and leadership experience without the overhead.

## What This Looks Like in Practice

Every engagement is different, but typical scenarios include:

- **A founder-led business** that needs help choosing between building custom software vs. buying SaaS tools
- **An SME** preparing for a technology investment and needing a technical strategy before hiring developers
- **A company** evaluating an acquisition target and needing honest technical due diligence
- **A growing team** that needs someone to define architecture standards and hiring processes

## How We Work Together

We start with a focused discovery session to understand your business, your current technology, and where you need to go. From there, we define a clear engagement scope - whether that's a one-time strategy project or ongoing advisory.

You always work directly with the principal consultant. No handoffs, no layers.
