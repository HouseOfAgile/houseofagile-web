## The Project

HeyBuddies ([heybuddi.es](https://heybuddi.es)) is a social platform that connects people through shared activities and local events. Unlike our consulting work, this is a product we built from scratch and continue to operate - giving us firsthand experience with the full lifecycle of running a live B2C platform.

## What We Built

- **Full-stack Symfony application** with PostgreSQL, handling user profiles, activity matching, event management, and messaging
- **Vue.js frontend** with real-time features, location-based discovery, and mobile-first responsive design
- **Payment integration** with Stripe for premium features and event ticketing
- **Operations infrastructure** on AWS with Docker, CI/CD pipelines, and monitoring

## Why This Matters

Running our own product keeps us sharp in ways that consulting alone doesn't:

- **We deal with real users** - not just stakeholders. We understand conversion funnels, retention, and the difference between features users say they want and features they actually use.
- **We pay the infrastructure bills** - so we genuinely care about cost optimization, not just theoretical best practices.
- **We ship continuously** - not quarterly releases, but daily iterations based on real usage data.

This hands-on product experience directly informs how we advise clients building their own platforms.
