The Challenge

Large-scale infrastructure inspections were costly and risky, and manual methods could not keep up with asset volume. The industry needed a safe, automated system to detect defects quickly and reliably.

The Solution

Built an autonomous drone platform combining hardware, AI models, and a robust data pipeline to detect defects in real time. The system automated inspections, accelerated reporting, and scaled safely across assets.
