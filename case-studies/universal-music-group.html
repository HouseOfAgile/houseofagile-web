Universal Music Group

Development factory platform managing all music, metadata, delivery and rights holders for Universal's global operations.

The Challenge

Led the complete overhaul of Universal's music metadata and delivery systems, managing multiple teams across different functions. The project required coordinating 120+ engineers across 5 development teams to build a unified platform for the world's largest music company.

The Solution

Architected and implemented a development factory platform managing all music/metadata/delivery and rights holders for Universal's global operations. The solution unified disparate systems into a cohesive platform serving Universal's worldwide distribution network.

Technical Implementation

Enterprise Architecture

  • Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA)
  • Enterprise Service Bus
  • Oracle Database Cluster
  • High-Availability Infrastructure

Data Management

  • Music Metadata Standards
  • Rights Management System
  • Digital Asset Management
  • Content Delivery Network

Team Leadership

  • 120+ Engineers Coordination
  • 5 Cross-functional Teams
  • Agile Transformation
  • Vendor Management

Results & Impact

Successfully delivered a global-scale platform that unified Universal Music Group's metadata and delivery systems. The platform enabled efficient management of music rights, streamlined content delivery to digital platforms worldwide, and established a foundation for Universal's digital transformation.
